# Diary Writing Templates

Use these templates as scaffolding. Keep real details from the user's day.

## Template A: Daily Reflection (balanced)

1) Opening moment
- What happened first that set today's tone?

2) Two key scenes
- Scene 1: event + feeling + what it revealed
- Scene 2: event + feeling + contrast with Scene 1

3) Perspective shift
- What did I notice about myself today?

4) Tomorrow action
- One specific action I will do tomorrow.

## Template B: Travel / Journey Day

1) Departure energy
- Why the trip felt heavy, light, or mixed.

2) On-the-road moments
- Keep 2-3 memory points (not full timeline).
- Include one concrete detail (price/place/time/object).

3) People and conversation
- What others said.
- What that stirred in me.

4) Return and grounding
- What changed after arriving.

5) Next-step commitment
- One practical habit or adjustment.

## Template C: Social Gathering Day

1) Context and expectation
- Why this gathering mattered today.

2) Key voices
- Summarize 2-3 people and one core issue each.

3) My position
- I mostly listened / I challenged / I stayed neutral.
- Why.

4) Reflection
- What part of their story mirrors my own life.

5) Action
- One behavior I will keep or change.

## Template D: Mini Entry (busy day)

- Today in one sentence
- One scene I do not want to forget
- One thing I understood better
- One small action for tomorrow

## Rewrite Pattern (from rough notes)

Convert each raw point using this pattern:

`event -> emotion -> interpretation -> action`

Example:
- Raw: "高铁上吃了 60 块饭"
- Revised: "在高铁上点了份 60 块的饭。以前我会嫌贵，现在更愿意在长途里照顾自己一点。这种变化让我看到，我对‘该省和该花’的边界在变清晰。"
